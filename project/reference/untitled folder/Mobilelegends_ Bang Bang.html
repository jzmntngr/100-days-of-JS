Rules
1. Event Time: May 12-21 (Server Time)

2. Players can control Harith to jump up in the game. By playing the game, you can earn 515 Coins, which can be used to redeem rewards. Landing on different part of the block grants different amount of 515 Coins. Landing at the center of the block grants the most 515 Coins. You stand a chance to trigger Super Jump and get more coins by landing at the center multiple times. You can get bonus reward when you get a total of 515 (five hundred and fifteen) coins in a single round of game. You can make it! You can earn up to 1500 coins per day. Once you reach the limit, you can still play games but earn no more coins. Logged-out players can only try the game without earning coins. Log in to play games and earn more coins.

3. Complete tasks to get Revival Card or Double Coin Card. Revival Card can be used to revive and continue when you lose the game. Double Coin Card can be used at the end of each game to double the coins you got. Both two types of card can be used only once for each round of game. If you quit the game before it ends or before activating the Revival Card/Double Coin Card, the game result will not be recorded.

4. Share the event link, or invite newly signed-up participants (higher than lv.8) or returning player (logged out for more than 30 days) to join the event to get Revival Card and Skateboard Chest. Whether a player is newly signed-up or returning is decided by the in-game data.

5. Open Skateboard Chest for a chance to get the item Skateboard Qualification Chest. The owner of Skateboard Qualification Chest will be qualified to be chosen to win the skateboard reward after the event ends. The more friends you invite, the more likely you'll be chosen. This chest will be automatically deleted after the event ends. You'll be informed via in-game mail if you're chosen.
If no players are chosen in the event, 5 event participants will be chosen.

6. The final result will be sent via in-game mail within 7 workdays after every event ends. Please submit your address information before 23:59, June 5, 2021, otherwise your reward will be discarded. The delivery may take 30 to 60 workdays. The delivery service is available only for Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, the Philippines, Myanmar, Cambodia, Japan, Brazil, Russia, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain, and Egypt. For players outside these regions, you can choose to ask your friends who live in the regions above for help, or convert the skateboard reward into 3000 Diamonds, which will be sent via in-game mail.

7. You need to meet some requirements to redeem rewards in 515 Exclusive Store. Tap the unlock button to check details. All participants can directly use 515 Coins to redeem rewards in Normal Store. Rewards will be sent to your in-game mailbox. There may be some delay, please wait in patience.

8. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang reserves the right of final interpretation of this event.
